HITL Required

Why HITL is the only legal model under AHPRA

Under AHPRA's professional standards and TGA Authorised Prescriber regulations, AI clinical decision support tools cannot replace prescriber judgment. Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) is not a design choice — it is the legal and ethical foundation of every HydraWell agent. All outputs require review and approval by an AHPRA-registered prescriber before any clinical action is taken.
